More questions?


  • Should my eyes be closed or opened? Some people experiences to be more relaxed with their eyes closed. Try what works best for you.
  • How should I breath? There is diffrent ways of breathing technics in meditation, easiest for beginnners is to get a guided meditation.
  • Is it normal to fall asleep? Probably everyone that have been practicing meditation for a while have feelt a sleep a couple of times in their journey.
  • What is best? Meditate by myself or in a group? To meditate in a group or have a guided meditation is great for beginners. Practicing meditation for yourself needs discipline but is a good skill to have when you need to meditate at work for example.
  • What should I do if I get distracted by my family, work..or the world? It is normal to get distracted here and there in your meditation. Just relax and get pack to your thoughts, it's a practice.
